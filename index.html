All content in this course is based directly on information from the College Board CED and AP Classroom.

AP Chemistry Exam Format

  • Section I: Multiple Choice
    • % of Score: 50%
    • Time Limit: 1 hour 30 minutes
    • Calculator Allowed: Yes
    • Formula Sheet Provided: Yes
    • Questions: 60 multiple-choice questions
  • Section II: Free Response
    • % of Score: 50%
    • Time Limit: 1 hour 45 minutes
    • Calculator Allowed: Yes
    • Formula Sheet Provided: Yes
    • Questions: 7 free-response (3 long, 4 short)

Details:
Both sections allow a scientific calculator and the official AP Chemistry formula sheet. Total exam time is roughly 3 hours and 15 minutes with a brief break between the multiple choice section and free response section.

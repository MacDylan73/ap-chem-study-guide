How AP Chemistry Scoring Works

  • The exam has two main sections:
    • Multiple Choice (MCQ): 60 questions, 90 minutes, 50% of total score
    • Free Response (FRQ): 7 questions (3 long, 4 short), 105 minutes, 50% of total score
  • Raw scores are converted to a scale from 1 to 5.
  • There is no penalty for guessing in MCQ.
  • Partial credit is awarded on FRQs if you show your work.
  • Check the College Board website for official scoring guidelines and recent cutoffs.
